# Research Objectives

## Primary Objective

To design an Agentic AI framework for intelligent patient monitoring and clinical decision support using the MIMIC-IV dataset.

---

## Specific Objectives

1. To investigate recent advancements in Agentic AI, Large Language Models, and intelligent healthcare systems through a comprehensive literature review.

2. To identify the limitations and research gaps in existing clinical decision support systems.

3. To develop a layered Agentic AI framework integrating memory management, reasoning, planning, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and multi-agent collaboration.

4. To design specialized intelligent agents responsible for patient monitoring, diagnosis support, risk prediction, treatment recommendation, and clinical explanation.

5. To incorporate trustworthy AI principles including explainability, transparency, safety, auditability, and human-in-the-loop validation.

6. To utilize the MIMIC-IV dataset as the primary data source for designing and validating the proposed framework.
